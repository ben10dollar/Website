WHS Chess Club

What's New:

Chess Club sign-ups will be on Wed, Sept 22 and with all other clubs, Fri, Sept 24 for Club Rush.

Make sure to come out to our table beside the metal containers to see what we have to offer!

Why:

Enjoy a lively conversation (or an intense tactical battle) with a buddy over an engaging game of chess.

No matter your level, you'll always find a worthy opponent here.

Discord:

We use Discord, and we know you do too ;)

Just click here join our server at no cost!

Where/When:

We almost always meet in the wonderfully air conditioned MAC A (Mr. Marchant's) room during lunch.

On Tues and Thurs, the boards will be laid out for anyone to jump in for a quick match.

Every other Tues, we'll also hold a short 10-15 min lesson on chess strategies along with a General Meeting status update!

Tournaments:

Occasionally, we host tournaments with other schools (Oxford, Sunny Hills).

So, if you'd like to broaden your opponent-base, try participating when they come around.

For the 2021-2022 school year, they'll be online at lichess.com.
