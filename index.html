Why:

Enjoy a lively conversation (or an intense tactical battle) with a buddy over an engaging game of chess.

No matter your level, you'll always find a worthy opponent at Chess Club.

If you'd like to join, please fill out our 2-question form here.

Discord:

We use Discord, and we know you do too ;)

Just click here join our server at no cost!

Where/When:

We almost always meet in the wonderfully air conditioned MAC A (Mr. Marchant's) room during lunch.

On Tues and Thurs, the boards will be laid out for anyone to jump in for a casual game.

Every other Tues, we'll also hold a short 10-15 min lesson on chess strategies/openings—join us to hone your positional and tactical skills!

Tournaments:

Occasionally, we host tournaments with other schools (Oxford, Sunny Hills).

So, if you'd like to broaden your opponent-base, try participating when they come around.

For the 2021-2022 school year, they'll be online at lichess.org.
