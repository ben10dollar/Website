A little about Chess Club:

We at WHS Chess Club aim to cultivate an environment for chess-lovers
of all levels, ranging from total newcomers to 4+ year veterans.

In accordance with our group goal, Chess Club was made to allow everyone to
unwind and enjoy a friendly conversation over a stimulating chess match or two,
so if that somewhat interests you, try it out for yourself!

You can find us in MAC A, where we host regular lessons weekly and
intra-club games daily at lunch in MAC A (check calendar tab for details).

Thanks for your time. If you'd like to become a member, fill out this 3 question
Google Form; if not, it's totally cool. make sure to drop in and say hi though! :D
