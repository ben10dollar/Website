A little about Chess Club:

We at Chess Club aim to cultivate an environment for chess-lovers
of all levels, ranging from total newcomers to 4+ year veterans.

Fulfilling our group goal, Chess Club allows everyone to
unwind and enjoy a friendly conversation over a stimulating chess match or two,
so if that interests you, try it out yourself!

You'll be able to find us in MAC A (Mr. Marchant's room), where we'll host regular lessons weekly and intra-club games daily at lunch (check calendar for details).

Thanks for your time. If you'd like to become a member, fill out the ultra-brief,
2 question Google Form below. If you're unable to, it's totally cool.

Make sure to drop in sometime and say hi though! :D
